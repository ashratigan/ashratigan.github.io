ABOUT


I am currently a Software Engineering Specialist at General Electric focusing on front-end development using React.

My background is in Geology and Archaeology with an emphasis on research and learning. Some of my previous experience includes conducting geology field research in remote North Slope, Alaska, cataloging a new paleontology collection at the American Museum of Natural History in New York, and mapping the biologic response to seismic disturbance at a lagoon in Christchurch, New Zealand.

In the summer of 2018, I completed General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive, where I continued to delve into computer science and compounded on my previous experience with python and statistics by learning web development. I am particularly interested in data visualization, and I’ve enjoyed creating projects with D3, React, and React libraries, such as React-Vis.
