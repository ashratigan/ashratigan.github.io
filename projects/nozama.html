About

Nozama was created as part of a group project with three other people. We spent our first day planning the project and breaking it down into different sections. We collaborated to create a wireframe, ERD, and user stories.

My role on the team was as back end lead. The back end was created using Node, MongoDB, Express, and Mongoose. The front end was created using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Handlebars.

In action

The app was based on Amazon, but with a lego figurine theme. Handlebars was used to display the items in the store.

Items can be added to the cart. Once an item is added, the cart total displayed in the nav bar increases to display the number of items in the cart. Clicking on the shopping cart displays the cart. To go back to viewing the items, click on the Nozama logo. The integration of Stripe is still in progress and proved to be the biggest challenge on this project.
